About us

Jaipur is a popular tourist destination in India and one of the most famous places for handicrafts. There are many famous handicrafts from Jaipur, including arts and crafts, heritage and diversity. The handicrafts of Jaipur are not just beautiful pieces of art but also have historical significance. Jaipur's handicrafts have a long history and are renowned for their intricate designs, exquisite craftsmanship, and quality. These handicrafts are not just limited to home decor but also include clothing, fashion, Indo western, accessories, and jewelry. Home decord includes rugs, cussions, sheets, curtains, lamp,chandelier, chairs, wooden furniture, mirrors etc.

What makes us special

  • Rich Cultural Heritage — Jaipur handicrafts are a product of the city's rich cultural heritage that dates back centuries. The city has a long history of artisans and craftsmen who have perfected their skills over generations, making Jaipur handicrafts some of the most unique and exquisite in the world.

  • Skilled Artisans — The artisans who create Jaipur handicrafts are highly skilled and have a deep understanding of traditional techniques and materials. They use a range of techniques like block printing, embroidery, and tie and dye to create intricate designs and patterns that are hard to find anywhere else.

  • Use of Natural Materials — Jaipur handicrafts are often made from natural materials like cotton, silk, and wool, which are locally sourced. These materials are not only sustainable and eco-friendly, but they also lend a unique texture and feel to the finished product.

  • Diverse Range of Products — Jaipur handicrafts encompass a wide range of products, including textiles, pottery, jewelry, and home decor. Each product is unique and showcases the skill and creativity of the artisans who made it.
