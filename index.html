Jaipuri Junction Antiques

The original handmade handicrafts

Jaipuri Junction Antiques is the one of the oldest originally handicraft shop located in the heart of pink city and is the leading center of townhall handicrafts market. The store has been run by the members of the original tribe who were also a part of creating beautiful masterpieces of art.

Jaipur is a name that brings to mind the vibrant colors and intricate embroidery of India. Arts and crafts from Jaipur are some of the most popular items in the world. They are made with a great deal of skill and care, and they often have a unique design. Some of the most famous arts and crafts from Jaipur include jharial (a type of brocade), bead crafted and stone work, lacquer work, metalwork, textiles, and wood carvings.

Jaipur Junction Antiques was established in 1957 by the local artists themselves. We pay homage to the tribes who have passed down their rich heritage of art and techniques to generations over decades.

Jaipur is a popular tourist destination in India and one of the most famous places for handicrafts. There are many famous handicrafts from Jaipur, including arts and crafts, heritage and diversity. They are not just beautiful pieces of art but also have historical significance. Jaipur's handicrafts have a long history and are renowned for their intricate designs, exquisite craftsmanship, and quality.
